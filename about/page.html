FAQs

What services do you offer?

I specialize in visual identity design, web design, photography, and graphic design. I often work on projects that combine these disciplines for a cohesive visual approach.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes Im based in France but work with clients worldwide. Most projects are handled remotely, and I adapt my process to different time zones.

How do you start a new project?

We begin with a consultation to discuss your goals, audience, and creative direction. From there, I prepare a proposal with a timeline, scope, and investment.

Can you handle both design and photography for the same project?

Absolutely. Many of my clients choose this integrated approach so their visual identity, website, and imagery feel consistent and aligned.

What is your pricing?

Each project is unique, so I provide a tailored quote after learning more about your needs. I work with both flat fees and project-based rates.

What if I only need small updates or one-off work?

I take on select smaller projects if they align with my style and availability. Just send me the details and Ill let you know if its a good fit.
